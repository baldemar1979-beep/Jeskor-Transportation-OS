# Trailer Pickup and Drop Procedure

## Purpose

This document establishes the standard process for trailer pickup and trailer drop operations.

---

# Pickup Process

## Step 1

Verify assignment.

Confirm:

- Trailer number
- Load information
- Pickup location

---

## Step 2

Inspect Trailer

Review:

- Visible damage
- Tire condition
- Lights
- Seal status

---

## Step 3

Connect Equipment

Verify:

- Fifth wheel connection
- Airlines
- Electrical connection

---

## Step 4

Conduct Tug Test

Confirm secure coupling.

---

## Step 5

Complete Documentation

Record required information.

---

# Drop Process

## Step 1

Verify destination.

---

## Step 2

Park trailer safely.

---

## Step 3

Set trailer brakes.

---

## Step 4

Disconnect equipment safely.

---

## Step 5

Complete required documentation.

---

# Damage Reporting

Immediately report:

- Equipment damage
- Missing seals
- Trailer defects

---

# Objective

Ensure safe and consistent trailer pickup and drop execution.
