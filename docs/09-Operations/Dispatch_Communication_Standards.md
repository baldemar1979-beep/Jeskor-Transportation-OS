# Dispatch Communication Standards

## Purpose

This document establishes communication expectations between drivers, dispatch personnel, and management.

The objective is to improve operational awareness and reduce communication failures.

## Communication Principles

* Be clear
* Be timely
* Be professional
* Be accurate

## Required Communications

Drivers shall report:

* Delays
* Equipment concerns
* Safety concerns
* Attendance issues
* Route disruptions

## Management Responsibilities

Management shall:

* Provide timely updates
* Communicate operational changes
* Respond to escalations
* Document significant issues

## Escalation Triggers

Escalate:

* Missed blocks
* Safety concerns
* Equipment failures
* Significant delays

## Documentation

Maintain records of significant operational communications.

## Objective

Support operational reliability through clear and consistent communication.
