# Operations KPI Framework

## Purpose

This document defines the key performance indicators used to evaluate operational performance.

---

# Service Metrics

## On-Time Departure

Track weekly and monthly.

Target:

98%+

---

## On-Time Arrival

Track weekly and monthly.

Target:

98%+

---

## Load Completion Rate

Track monthly.

Target:

99%+

---

# Driver Metrics

Track:

- Attendance
- Schedule adherence
- Communication compliance

---

# Equipment Metrics

Track:

- Tractor utilization
- Trailer utilization
- Out-of-service equipment

---

# Service Recovery Metrics

Track:

- Missed loads
- Service failures
- Recovery time

---

# Operational Reviews

Weekly:

- Open issues
- Missed loads
- Equipment concerns

Monthly:

- KPI performance
- Trend analysis
- Improvement opportunities

---

# Objective

Measure operational effectiveness and support continuous improvement.
