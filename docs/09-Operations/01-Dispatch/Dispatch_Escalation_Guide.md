# Dispatch Escalation Guide

## Purpose

This document establishes escalation procedures for operational events requiring management involvement.

---

# Escalation Philosophy

Problems should be escalated early rather than after service failure occurs.

The objective is rapid issue resolution and service protection.

---

# Level 1 Escalations

Examples:

- Minor delay
- Customer communication request
- Equipment concern
- Driver scheduling conflict

Response:

- Dispatch resolves

---

# Level 2 Escalations

Examples:

- Load at risk
- Significant delay
- Driver no response
- Equipment breakdown

Response:

- Dispatch Manager notified

---

# Level 3 Escalations

Examples:

- Missed load
- Accident
- Driver injury
- Customer escalation

Response:

- Operations Manager notified

---

# Level 4 Escalations

Examples:

- Serious accident
- Major customer impact
- Multiple service failures
- Media involvement

Response:

- Ownership notified

---

# Documentation

Record:

- Event
- Escalation Level
- Actions Taken
- Resolution

---

# Objective

Resolve issues before they become service failures.
