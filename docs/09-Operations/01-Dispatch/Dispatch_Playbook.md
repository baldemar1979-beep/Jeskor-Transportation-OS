# Dispatch Playbook

## Purpose

This document establishes the standard operating procedures for dispatch operations.

The objective is to ensure safe, efficient, and reliable load execution while maintaining strong communication with drivers and customers.

---

# Dispatch Responsibilities

Dispatch is responsible for:

- Load management
- Driver communication
- Schedule management
- Service recovery
- Escalation management

---

# Daily Dispatch Routine

## Start of Shift

Review:

- Available drivers
- Available equipment
- Scheduled loads
- Open issues

---

## During Shift

Monitor:

- Load execution
- Driver communication
- Delays
- Safety concerns

---

## End of Shift

Review:

- Completed loads
- Open loads
- Driver concerns
- Escalations

---

# Driver Communication Standards

Respond to driver inquiries promptly.

Provide:

- Clear instructions
- Accurate information
- Professional communication

---

# Escalation Triggers

Examples:

- Missed loads
- Equipment breakdowns
- Accidents
- Driver illness
- Severe weather

---

# Documentation

Maintain records of:

- Load issues
- Driver communications
- Escalations
- Service failures

---

# Performance Objectives

- Safe operations
- On-time service
- Professional communication
- Effective problem resolution
