# Yard Management Process

## Purpose

This document establishes procedures for managing yard operations, trailer inventory, and equipment movement.

---

# Objectives

- Maintain yard organization
- Improve trailer visibility
- Reduce delays
- Improve safety

---

# Trailer Management

Track:

- Loaded trailers
- Empty trailers
- Out-of-service trailers
- Reserved trailers

---

# Yard Organization

Maintain designated areas for:

- Loaded equipment
- Empty equipment
- Maintenance equipment
- Quarantine equipment

---

# Daily Yard Review

Verify:

- Trailer locations
- Equipment condition
- Inventory accuracy

---

# Damage Reporting

Immediately report:

- Trailer damage
- Facility damage
- Equipment issues

---

# Yard Safety

Enforce:

- Speed limits
- PPE requirements
- Safe backing procedures

---

# Objective

Maintain safe, organized, and efficient yard operations.
