# Equipment Assignment Process

## Purpose

This document establishes the process for assigning tractors, trailers, and other equipment.

---

# Objectives

- Maximize equipment utilization
- Improve accountability
- Reduce equipment damage
- Support maintenance planning

---

# Assignment Principles

Equipment assignments should consider:

- Driver qualifications
- Equipment availability
- Maintenance status
- Operational requirements

---

# Assignment Process

## Step 1

Verify equipment availability.

---

## Step 2

Verify maintenance status.

---

## Step 3

Assign equipment.

---

## Step 4

Document assignment.

---

## Step 5

Confirm driver acceptance.

---

# Equipment Tracking

Maintain records of:

- Assigned driver
- Unit number
- Assignment date
- Return date

---

# Exceptions

Immediately report:

- Damage
- Mechanical concerns
- Missing equipment
- Unauthorized usage

---

# Objective

Ensure equipment is assigned consistently and tracked accurately.
