# Equipment Breakdown Response Plan

## Purpose

This document establishes the response process for tractor and trailer breakdowns.

---

# Objectives

- Protect driver safety
- Minimize downtime
- Protect customer commitments
- Restore service quickly

---

# Driver Actions

Immediately:

- Move to a safe location
- Notify dispatch
- Provide equipment information
- Provide location

---

# Dispatch Actions

Determine:

- Driver status
- Equipment status
- Load impact
- Recovery options

---

# Recovery Options

Examples:

- Roadside service
- Replacement tractor
- Replacement trailer
- Load transfer

---

# Escalation

Notify management if:

- Load is at risk
- Driver safety is impacted
- Customer commitments are impacted

---

# Documentation

Record:

- Equipment
- Breakdown type
- Downtime
- Recovery action
- Cost

---

# Continuous Improvement

Review breakdown trends monthly.
