# Relay Yard Operations

## Purpose

This document establishes procedures for operating within Amazon relay yards and partner facilities.

The objective is to ensure safe, efficient, and professional execution of relay operations.

---

# Driver Expectations

Drivers shall:

- Follow site rules
- Follow posted speed limits
- Wear required PPE
- Respect facility personnel
- Report safety concerns

---

# Arrival Procedures

Upon arrival:

- Verify facility location
- Follow check-in procedures
- Review load assignment
- Confirm trailer number

---

# Yard Movement

Drivers shall:

- Operate at safe speeds
- Use caution around pedestrians
- Follow designated traffic flow
- Minimize unnecessary movement

---

# Trailer Identification

Verify:

- Trailer number
- Load assignment
- Seal status
- Visible damage

Before movement.

---

# Departure Procedures

Before leaving:

- Confirm trailer assignment
- Verify secure connection
- Verify lights
- Verify documentation

---

# Safety Requirements

Immediately report:

- Yard accidents
- Property damage
- Unsafe conditions
- Security concerns

---

# Objective

Maintain safe and efficient relay yard operations while protecting company reputation and customer relationships.
