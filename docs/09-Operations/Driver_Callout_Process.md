# Driver Callout Process

## Purpose

This document establishes procedures for managing driver absences and call-outs.

The objective is to maintain operational coverage while supporting accountability and communication.

## Reporting Expectations

Drivers shall notify management as soon as possible when unable to report for duty.

## Required Information

Drivers shall provide:

* Name
* Scheduled shift
* Reason for absence
* Expected return date if known

## Management Responsibilities

Management shall:

* Confirm absence
* Review coverage options
* Document call-out
* Update schedules

## Coverage Planning

Potential coverage options include:

* Reserve drivers
* Schedule adjustments
* Operational contingencies

## Documentation

Maintain:

* Driver name
* Date
* Shift affected
* Reason provided
* Coverage actions

## Objective

Manage attendance issues consistently while minimizing operational disruptions.
