# Peak Season Operations Plan

## Purpose

This document establishes planning procedures for periods of increased operational demand.

---

# Objectives

- Maintain service levels
- Protect safety performance
- Improve resource planning

---

# Peak Season Planning

Review:

- Forecasted demand
- Driver availability
- Equipment availability
- Maintenance schedules

---

# Staffing Plan

Prepare:

- Additional driver capacity
- Overtime planning
- Coverage plans

---

# Equipment Plan

Verify:

- Tractor readiness
- Trailer readiness
- Maintenance completion

---

# Communication Plan

Increase communication frequency between:

- Dispatch
- Drivers
- Safety
- Management

---

# Risk Areas

Monitor:

- Fatigue
- Missed loads
- Equipment failures
- Staffing shortages

---

# Post-Peak Review

Review:

- Performance
- Lessons learned
- Improvement opportunities

---

# Objective

Execute peak operations safely and efficiently while maintaining customer service expectations.
