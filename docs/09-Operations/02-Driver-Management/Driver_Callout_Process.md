# Driver Callout Process

## Purpose

This document establishes procedures for managing driver absences and callouts.

---

# Objectives

- Maintain operational continuity
- Protect customer service
- Minimize disruptions

---

# Driver Responsibilities

Drivers shall notify management as soon as practical.

Provide:

- Reason for absence
- Expected return date
- Supporting information if required

---

# Management Response

Review:

- Route impact
- Coverage options
- Equipment assignments

---

# Coverage Process

Priority Order:

1. Available scheduled drivers
2. Standby drivers
3. Overtime opportunities
4. Management escalation

---

# Documentation

Record:

- Driver
- Date
- Reason
- Coverage Action

---

# Attendance Tracking

Monitor:

- Frequency
- Patterns
- Repeat occurrences

---

# Objective

Ensure operational stability while supporting employees appropriately.
