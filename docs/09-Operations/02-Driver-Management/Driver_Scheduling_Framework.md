# Driver Scheduling Framework

## Purpose

This document establishes the scheduling methodology used for assigning drivers to routes and shifts.

The objective is to maximize service reliability while maintaining compliance, driver satisfaction, and operational efficiency.

---

# Scheduling Principles

Scheduling should prioritize:

1. Safety
2. Hours of Service Compliance
3. Customer Commitments
4. Driver Availability
5. Operational Efficiency

---

# Driver Availability

Track:

- Scheduled workdays
- Time off requests
- Vacation schedules
- Medical restrictions
- Training schedules

---

# Schedule Planning

Review weekly:

- Route demand
- Driver availability
- Equipment availability
- Peak demand periods

---

# Coverage Planning

Maintain contingency plans for:

- Sick calls
- Vacation coverage
- Equipment failures
- Driver shortages

---

# Schedule Changes

Communicate schedule changes as early as possible.

Document:

- Reason for change
- Date communicated
- Driver acknowledgment

---

# Compliance

Ensure:

- Hours of Service compliance
- Required rest periods
- Company scheduling policies

---

# Objective

Provide reliable driver coverage while supporting safe operations and driver retention.
