# Operations Management Philosophy

## Purpose

This document establishes Jeskor Transportation's operations management philosophy.

The objective is to safely and consistently execute all assigned Amazon Freight Partner blocks while maintaining high operational performance.

## Core Principles

### Safety First

No operational objective outweighs safety.

### Reliable Execution

Every assigned block should be completed as planned whenever possible.

### Accountability

Operational issues require ownership and timely resolution.

### Communication

Clear communication supports effective decision making and operational execution.

### Continuous Improvement

Operations should be reviewed regularly to identify opportunities for improvement.

## Operational Objectives

* Maximize block completion
* Reduce missed blocks
* Improve attendance
* Improve dispatch effectiveness
* Reduce service disruptions
* Support profitable growth

## Key Performance Indicators

Monitor:

* Block completion rate
* Missed block rate
* Driver attendance
* Late departures
* Service failures
* Operational escalations

## Objective

Maintain a reliable operation capable of supporting long-term AFP success.
