# Daily Operations Process

## Purpose

This document establishes Jeskor Transportation's daily operational management process.

The objective is to ensure all blocks are staffed, tractors are available, and operational risks are identified before impacting service.

## Daily Review Process

### Staffing Review

Verify:

* Driver availability
* Attendance concerns
* Schedule coverage

### Equipment Review

Verify:

* Tractor availability
* Open maintenance issues
* Out-of-service vehicles

### Block Review

Verify:

* Assigned blocks
* Start times
* Coverage plans

### Risk Review

Identify:

* Potential call-outs
* Equipment concerns
* Operational constraints

## Shift Monitoring

Monitor:

* Driver check-ins
* Block departures
* Operational delays
* Escalations

## End-of-Day Review

Review:

* Missed blocks
* Attendance issues
* Equipment concerns
* Lessons learned

## Objective

Create a repeatable process that supports reliable daily operations.
