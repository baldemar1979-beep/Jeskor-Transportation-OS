# Compliance Department

## Purpose

The Compliance Department serves as the central repository for regulatory compliance, driver qualification management, drug and alcohol program administration, FMCSA requirements, Amazon-specific compliance obligations, audit readiness, and qualification management.

The objective is to maintain full compliance with federal regulations, Amazon Freight Partner requirements, and company policies while reducing regulatory risk.

---

# Department Structure

## 01-DQ-Files

Driver Qualification File management.

## 02-Drug-and-Alcohol

Drug and alcohol testing programs.

## 03-FMCSA

FMCSA regulatory requirements.

## 04-Amazon-Requirements

Amazon-specific compliance requirements.

## 05-Audits

Internal and external audit programs.

## 06-Training-and-Qualifications

Training records and qualification management.

## 07-Summaries

Lessons learned, best practices, and executive summaries.

---

# Compliance Philosophy

Compliance is a daily operational responsibility, not an annual event.

---

# Long-Term Objective

Develop a compliance management system that maintains regulatory readiness, supports safe operations, and withstands internal, external, and customer audits.
