# Medical Card Tracking Program

## Purpose

This document establishes procedures for monitoring and maintaining driver medical certification compliance.

---

# Objectives

- Maintain driver qualification
- Prevent expired medical certifications
- Reduce compliance risk

---

# Medical Certificate Requirements

All CDL drivers must maintain a valid Medical Examiner's Certificate as required by regulation.

---

# Tracking Process

Track:

- Driver name
- Medical card expiration date
- Restriction status
- Medical examiner information

---

# Notification Schedule

## 90 Days Prior

Initial reminder.

---

## 60 Days Prior

Second reminder.

---

## 30 Days Prior

Final reminder.

---

## Expiration Date

Driver may not operate if qualification requirements are not met.

---

# Documentation

Maintain:

- Medical card copy
- Tracking records
- Driver notifications

---

# Audit Readiness

Maintain records in organized and accessible format.

---

# Objective

Ensure continuous medical qualification for all drivers.
