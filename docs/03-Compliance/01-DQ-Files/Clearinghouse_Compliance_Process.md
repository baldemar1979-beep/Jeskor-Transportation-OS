# Clearinghouse Compliance Process

## Purpose

This document establishes procedures for complying with FMCSA Drug and Alcohol Clearinghouse requirements.

---

# Objectives

- Verify driver eligibility
- Maintain compliance
- Prevent prohibited driver operation

---

# Pre-Employment Requirements

Prior to hire:

- Obtain driver consent
- Conduct Clearinghouse query
- Review results

---

# Annual Requirements

Conduct annual limited query for all CDL drivers.

Maintain documentation.

---

# Prohibited Drivers

Drivers identified as prohibited shall not operate a commercial motor vehicle until Return-to-Duty requirements are satisfied.

---

# Documentation

Maintain:

- Query records
- Consent records
- Results documentation

---

# Audit Readiness

Maintain records in an organized and accessible format.

---

# Objective

Ensure no driver operates in violation of FMCSA Clearinghouse requirements.
