# Annual MVR Review Process

## Purpose

This document establishes procedures for annual Motor Vehicle Record reviews.

---

# Objectives

- Identify elevated driver risk
- Verify qualification status
- Maintain regulatory compliance

---

# Review Frequency

Conduct annually for all CDL drivers.

---

# Review Process

## Step 1

Obtain current MVR.

---

## Step 2

Review violations.

---

## Step 3

Review accident history.

---

## Step 4

Determine qualification status.

---

## Step 5

Document findings.

---

# Review Outcomes

## Qualified

Driver remains qualified.

---

## Conditional Review

Additional management review required.

---

## Disqualified

Driver no longer meets qualification standards.

---

# Documentation

Maintain:

- MVR copy
- Review notes
- Qualification determination

---

# Objective

Maintain qualified drivers and reduce fleet risk.
