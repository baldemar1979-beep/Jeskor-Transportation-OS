# Drug and Alcohol Testing Program

## Purpose

This document establishes the DOT drug and alcohol testing program.

---

# Testing Types

## Pre-Employment

Required before safety-sensitive duties.

---

## Random

Conduct according to DOT requirements.

---

## Post-Accident

Conduct when required by regulation.

---

## Reasonable Suspicion

Conduct when observations support testing.

---

## Return-to-Duty

Conduct following required SAP process.

---

## Follow-Up

Conduct according to SAP requirements.

---

# Responsibilities

Management shall:

- Maintain consortium relationship
- Schedule required testing
- Maintain records

---

# Documentation

Maintain:

- Test records
- Chain of custody records
- Consortium documentation

---

# Confidentiality

Protect all testing records according to applicable requirements.

---

# Objective

Maintain full compliance with DOT drug and alcohol regulations.
