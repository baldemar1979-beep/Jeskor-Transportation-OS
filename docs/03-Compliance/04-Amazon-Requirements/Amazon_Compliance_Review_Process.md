# Amazon Compliance Review Process

## Purpose

This document establishes the process for reviewing Amazon Freight Partner compliance requirements and performance expectations.

---

# Objectives

- Maintain AFP eligibility
- Identify compliance risks
- Improve operational performance
- Protect growth opportunities

---

# Review Frequency

Conduct monthly reviews.

Conduct additional reviews following major incidents or program updates.

---

# Review Categories

## Safety

Review:

- Safety scorecards
- Netradyne metrics
- Accident performance
- Coaching completion

---

## Operations

Review:

- Missed blocks
- Service failures
- Route execution performance

---

## Compliance

Review:

- DQ files
- Medical cards
- Drug and alcohol compliance
- Required training

---

## Driver Performance

Review:

- Attendance
- Coaching history
- Safety events
- Qualification status

---

# Corrective Actions

Document:

- Deficiency
- Owner
- Due date
- Completion status

---

# Documentation

Maintain records of:

- Reviews completed
- Findings
- Corrective actions
- Closure dates

---

# Objective

Maintain continuous compliance with Amazon Freight Partner program expectations.
