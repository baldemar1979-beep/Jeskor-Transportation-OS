# Amazon Enforcement Framework

## Purpose

This document establishes Jeskor Transportation's understanding of Amazon Freight Partner enforcement actions, escalation pathways, and corrective action expectations.

The objective is to proactively identify compliance risks and prevent enforcement actions that may impact growth eligibility, financial incentives, or AFP participation.

---

# Enforcement Philosophy

Amazon generally evaluates:

- Severity
- Frequency
- Repeat behavior
- Failure to correct

Single events may not trigger enforcement.

Patterns and failure to improve increase enforcement exposure.

---

# Enforcement Escalation Path

## Level 1

Courtesy Notification

Purpose:

- Awareness
- Early intervention
- Corrective action opportunity

---

## Level 2

Corrective Action Plan (CAP)

Purpose:

- Formal improvement plan
- Defined corrective actions
- Increased monitoring

---

## Level 3

Business Operations Review (BOR)

Purpose:

- Leadership review
- Program performance assessment
- Elevated enforcement risk

---

## Level 4

Offboarding Risk

Purpose:

- Significant compliance failure
- Program eligibility concern
- Contract risk

---

# High Risk Categories

Examples:

- Driver qualification failures
- Drug and alcohol violations
- Safety program deficiencies
- Missed block trends
- Service failures
- Insurance issues
- Regulatory non-compliance

---

# Management Expectations

Leadership shall:

- Review enforcement notifications
- Track corrective actions
- Verify completion
- Monitor recurring issues

---

# Corrective Action Tracking

Maintain:

- Issue
- Date identified
- Owner
- Due date
- Completion status

---

# Objective

Maintain AFP compliance while preventing escalation to formal enforcement actions.
