# Compliance Calendar Framework

## Purpose

This document establishes the compliance calendar used to manage recurring compliance obligations.

---

# Objectives

- Prevent missed requirements
- Improve planning
- Maintain audit readiness

---

# Monthly Reviews

Review:

- Medical card expirations
- Driver qualification files
- Training requirements
- Compliance deficiencies

---

# Quarterly Reviews

Review:

- DQ audits
- Policy reviews
- Compliance metrics

---

# Annual Reviews

Review:

- MVR reviews
- Clearinghouse queries
- Driver qualification reviews
- Program effectiveness

---

# Calendar Ownership

Compliance leadership is responsible for maintaining the calendar.

---

# Documentation

Maintain records of completed reviews and corrective actions.

---

# Objective

Create a structured process for managing compliance obligations.
