# Driver Qualification Audit Process

## Purpose

This document establishes procedures for auditing Driver Qualification Files.

---

# Objectives

- Verify compliance
- Identify deficiencies
- Maintain audit readiness

---

# Audit Frequency

Conduct quarterly audits.

Additional audits may be conducted as needed.

---

# Audit Scope

Review:

- Employment application
- CDL copy
- Medical certificate
- MVR reviews
- Clearinghouse records
- Safety performance history

---

# Audit Outcomes

## Compliant

No deficiencies identified.

---

## Minor Deficiency

Correction required.

---

## Major Deficiency

Immediate corrective action required.

---

# Corrective Actions

Document:

- Deficiency
- Responsible party
- Due date
- Completion date

---

# Documentation

Maintain audit records and corrective action tracking.

---

# Objective

Maintain complete and compliant Driver Qualification Files.
