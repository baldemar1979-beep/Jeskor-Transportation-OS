# Amazon Enforcement Library

## Purpose

This repository captures key lessons learned from Amazon Freight Partner enforcement guidance, safety enforcement updates, compliance expectations, scorecard changes, corrective action trends, and operational reviews.

The objective is to transform Amazon documentation into actionable business knowledge that can be incorporated into Jeskor Transportation's operating system.

---

# Repository Contents

## Enforcement Matrix Reviews

Summaries of Amazon enforcement matrices and escalation frameworks.

---

## Safety Enforcement Reviews

Summaries of safety enforcement expectations, corrective actions, and operational lessons learned.

---

## Corrective Action Plan Reviews

Lessons learned from CAPs, root causes, corrective actions, and prevention strategies.

---

## Scorecard Reviews

Reviews of AFP scorecards, performance trends, metric changes, and operational impacts.

---

## Program Updates

Reviews of AFP announcements, operational changes, and compliance expectations.

---

# Lessons Learned Philosophy

Amazon documents should not simply be stored.

They should be reviewed, summarized, and incorporated into operating procedures and management practices.

Use this repository to document:
