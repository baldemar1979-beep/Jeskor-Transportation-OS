# Q2 2025 Safety Enforcement Insights

## Source

AFP Safety Enforcement Guidance

## Purpose

This document captures key lessons learned from Amazon Freight Partner safety enforcement guidance and identifies actions Jeskor Transportation can take to proactively manage safety risk.

## Key Observation

Amazon evaluates both:

* Individual driver behavior
* Management response effectiveness

Failure to address recurring safety issues increases enforcement exposure.

## High-Risk Safety Categories

### Preventable Crashes

Monitor:

* Frequency
* Severity
* Root causes
* Corrective action effectiveness

### Failure to Report Incidents

Examples:

* Delayed reporting
* Missing documentation
* Incomplete investigations

### Safety Technology Tampering

Examples:

* Netradyne obstruction
* Device tampering
* Unauthorized modifications

### Training Deficiencies

Examples:

* Missed safety training
* Incomplete certifications
* Expired qualifications

### Investigation Non-Cooperation

Drivers and management are expected to fully cooperate with investigations and corrective action reviews.

## Lessons Learned

### Lesson 1

Complete coaching promptly.

Delayed coaching increases enforcement exposure.

### Lesson 2

Investigate every safety event.

Thorough investigations support effective corrective actions.

### Lesson 3

Document corrective actions.

Corrective actions should be retained and available for review.

### Lesson 4

Monitor repeat offenders.

Repeated safety events indicate elevated operational risk.

### Lesson 5

Maintain training compliance.

Incomplete training creates avoidable enforcement exposure.

## Jeskor Transportation Applications

Future management systems should track:

* Repeat safety events
* Open investigations
* Coaching completion
* Training compliance
* Enforcement exposure trends

## Future Dashboard Recommendations

Monitor:

* Preventable crashes
* Open investigations
* Coaching completion rates
* Safety event trends
* Training completion status

## Strategic Takeaway

Amazon evaluates both driver performance and management effectiveness. Strong documentation, timely coaching, and proactive intervention reduce enforcement exposure and improve safety performance.

## Conclusion

Jeskor Transportation should focus on proactive safety management, coaching completion, corrective action tracking, and training compliance to reduce safety enforcement risk and support long-term AFP success.
