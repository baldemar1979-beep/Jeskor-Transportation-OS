# Amazon Enforcement Key Principles

## Purpose

This document summarizes key enforcement principles identified through Amazon Freight Partner enforcement guidance and compliance reviews.

## Principle 1

Amazon evaluates trends more than isolated events.

## Principle 2

Corrective actions must address root causes.

## Principle 3

Documentation is critical.

## Principle 4

Leadership accountability increases with risk severity.

## Principle 5

Failure to correct recurring issues increases enforcement exposure.

## Principle 6

Business controls are evaluated alongside operational performance.

## Principle 7

Evidence of corrective action effectiveness is expected.

## Principle 8

Safety, compliance, finance, and operations are interconnected.

## Jeskor Application

Management systems should focus on:

- Early detection
- Corrective action ownership
- Trend monitoring
- Continuous improvement

## Objective

Create a concise reference guide for understanding Amazon's enforcement philosophy.
