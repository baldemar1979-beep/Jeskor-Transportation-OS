# Lessons Learned

## Purpose

The Lessons Learned Department serves as the central repository for institutional knowledge, operational insights, audit findings, enforcement updates, and continuous improvement opportunities.

The objective is to capture critical lessons from Amazon Freight Partner operations, safety programs, compliance reviews, operational events, and business growth activities.

---

# Department Structure

## Amazon-Enforcement-Library

Contains summaries of Amazon enforcement updates, compliance expectations, safety enforcement guidance, scorecard changes, and corrective action trends.

---

# Lessons Learned Philosophy

Knowledge gained through experience should be documented and shared to prevent recurring issues and improve organizational performance.

---

# Usage

Use this department to document:

- Enforcement updates
- Audit findings
- Major incidents
- Program improvements
- Operational best practices
- Compliance insights
- Safety lessons learned

---

# Long-Term Objective

Create a permanent organizational knowledge library that allows Jeskor Transportation to continuously improve and preserve operational expertise as the company grows.
