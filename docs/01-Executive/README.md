# Executive

The Executive Department serves as the strategic leadership and decision-making center of Jeskor Transportation.

## Responsibilities

- Company Vision
- Strategic Planning
- Growth Planning
- Annual Goals
- Business Planning
- Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Major Company Decisions
- Operating System Development

## Key Documents

- Business Plan
- JTOS Project Tracker
- Annual Objectives
- Strategic Initiatives
- Organizational Structure

## Purpose

This department acts as the central leadership hub for Jeskor Transportation and provides direction for all other departments.
