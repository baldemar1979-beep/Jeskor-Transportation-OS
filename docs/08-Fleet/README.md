# Maintenance

## Purpose

The Maintenance Department manages preventive maintenance activities, repair programs, service schedules, maintenance records, and vehicle reliability initiatives.

The objective is to maximize tractor uptime, reduce breakdowns, maintain compliance, and support safe operations.

## Department Contents

### Preventive Maintenance Program

Defines maintenance scheduling, inspection requirements, and service expectations.

### Maintenance Records

Maintains repair history, invoices, service documentation, and maintenance tracking.

### Reliability Management

Identifies recurring failures and improvement opportunities.

## Key Objectives

* Reduce breakdowns
* Improve uptime
* Maintain compliance
* Control maintenance costs
* Improve vehicle reliability

## Success Metrics

Monitor:

* PM compliance
* Breakdown frequency
* Repair costs
* Downtime hours
* Vehicle availability
