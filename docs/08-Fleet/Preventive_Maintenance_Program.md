# Preventive Maintenance Program

## Purpose

This document establishes Jeskor Transportation's preventive maintenance program for company tractors.

The objective is to maximize uptime, reduce breakdowns, maintain compliance, and support safe operations.

## Maintenance Philosophy

Maintenance shall be proactive rather than reactive.

Preventive maintenance reduces downtime, repair costs, and operational disruptions.

## Maintenance Scheduling

Maintenance intervals shall follow:

* OEM recommendations
* Leasing company requirements
* Vendor recommendations
* Operational experience

## Maintenance Categories

### Scheduled PM

Includes:

* Oil changes
* Fluid inspections
* Filter replacement
* Brake inspections
* Tire inspections

### Safety Inspections

Includes:

* Lighting
* Brakes
* Steering
* Suspension
* Safety equipment

## Responsibilities

### Drivers

* Complete DVIR inspections
* Report defects immediately
* Report warning lights or abnormal vehicle behavior

### Management

* Schedule maintenance
* Track PM compliance
* Maintain maintenance records
* Review recurring repair trends

## Documentation

Maintain:

* PM records
* Repair invoices
* Inspection records
* Vendor reports

## Performance Metrics

Monitor:

* PM compliance rate
* Breakdown frequency
* Downtime hours
* Maintenance costs
* Repeat repairs

## Objective

Maintain reliable tractors capable of supporting safe and uninterrupted AFP operations.
