# Recurring Repair Analysis

## Purpose

This document establishes a process for identifying recurring tractor defects and repeat repair failures.

The objective is to eliminate chronic equipment issues and improve fleet reliability.

## Review Frequency

Review monthly.

Additional reviews should occur following major breakdowns or repeat failures.

## Review Criteria

Evaluate:

* Repeat repairs
* Multiple repairs on the same component
* Vendor repair quality concerns
* Recurring warning lights
* Roadside failures

## Root Cause Analysis

Determine:

* What failed
* Why it failed
* Whether previous repairs addressed the issue
* Whether preventive measures exist

## Corrective Actions

Potential actions include:

* Additional inspections
* Vendor review
* Replacement of defective components
* Maintenance schedule changes
* Driver coaching

## Documentation

Maintain:

* Repair history
* Vendor records
* Breakdown reports
* Root cause findings
* Corrective actions

## Success Metrics

Monitor:

* Repeat failures
* Repeat repairs
* Downtime reduction
* Cost reduction

## Objective

Identify and eliminate recurring defects before they impact operations.
