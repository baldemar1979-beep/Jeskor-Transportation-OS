# Breakdown Response Process

## Purpose

This document establishes procedures for responding to tractor breakdowns and roadside events.

The objective is to minimize downtime, protect driver safety, and maintain operational continuity.

## Immediate Priorities

1. Protect driver safety
2. Secure vehicle location
3. Notify management
4. Coordinate repair services
5. Protect assigned block execution

## Driver Responsibilities

Drivers shall:

* Move to a safe location when possible
* Activate emergency equipment
* Contact management immediately
* Provide accurate location information
* Describe the issue clearly

## Management Responsibilities

Management shall:

* Confirm driver safety
* Assess operational impact
* Coordinate repairs
* Determine replacement coverage if available
* Communicate status updates
* Document the event

## Repair Options

Evaluate:

* Mobile repair
* Towing
* Shop repair
* Replacement tractor

## Documentation

Maintain:

* Breakdown report
* Tractor number
* Driver name
* Date and time
* Location
* Vendor used
* Downtime
* Repair invoice

## Performance Metrics

Monitor:

* Breakdown frequency
* Downtime hours
* Repair response times
* Repeat failures

## Objective

Restore service quickly while maintaining safety and minimizing operational disruption.
