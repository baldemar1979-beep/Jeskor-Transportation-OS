# Fleet Management Philosophy

## Purpose

This document establishes Jeskor Transportation's fleet management philosophy for company tractors.

The objective is to maximize uptime, maintain compliance, reduce operating costs, and support safe Amazon Freight Partner operations.

## Fleet Scope

Jeskor Transportation manages tractors only.

Trailer ownership, trailer maintenance, trailer inventory, and trailer repair responsibilities remain outside Jeskor Transportation's fleet management program.

## Core Principles

### Safety First

No operational objective outweighs safety.

### Uptime Matters

Reliable tractors support revenue generation, scorecard performance, and customer service.

### Preventive Maintenance

Preventive maintenance is preferred over reactive repairs whenever possible.

### Driver Partnership

Drivers are the first line of defense in identifying defects and maintenance concerns.

### Accountability

Fleet performance requires ownership, documentation, and follow-through.

## Fleet Objectives

* Maximize tractor availability
* Reduce breakdowns
* Maintain regulatory compliance
* Improve maintenance planning
* Reduce roadside events
* Support safe operations

## Key Performance Indicators

Monitor:

* Tractor uptime
* PM compliance
* Breakdown frequency
* Downtime hours
* Roadside repair events
* Maintenance cost per tractor

## Management Responsibilities

Management shall:

* Maintain fleet records
* Track maintenance compliance
* Manage repair vendors
* Monitor performance trends
* Support continuous improvement

## Continuous Improvement

Fleet performance shall be reviewed regularly to identify opportunities to improve reliability, safety, and operational efficiency.

## Objective

Maintain a reliable tractor fleet capable of supporting safe, consistent, and profitable AFP operations.
