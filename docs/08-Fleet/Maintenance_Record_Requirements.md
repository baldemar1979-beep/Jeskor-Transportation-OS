# Maintenance Record Requirements

## Purpose

This document establishes maintenance documentation requirements for Jeskor Transportation tractors.

The objective is to maintain accurate records that support compliance, audits, maintenance planning, and operational decision-making.

## Required Records

Maintain:

* Preventive maintenance records
* Repair invoices
* DVIR repair verification
* Vendor service reports
* Tire replacement records
* Roadside repair records

## Record Retention

Records shall be maintained in an organized and accessible format.

Electronic storage is preferred whenever possible.

## Documentation Standards

Records should include:

* Tractor number
* Date of service
* Vendor name
* Description of work performed
* Cost of repair
* Supporting documentation

## Audit Readiness

Maintenance records should be readily available for:

* Internal reviews
* Vendor discussions
* Compliance reviews
* Insurance requests

## Responsibilities

Management shall:

* Maintain records
* Verify completeness
* Organize documentation
* Retain supporting evidence

## Success Metrics

Monitor:

* Missing records
* Incomplete records
* Audit findings
* Documentation compliance

## Objective

Maintain accurate maintenance records that support operational excellence and compliance.
