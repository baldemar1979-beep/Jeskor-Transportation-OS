# Out of Service Vehicle Process

## Purpose

This document establishes procedures for managing tractors that have been determined to be unsafe or unavailable for operation.

## Out of Service Criteria

Examples include:

* Brake defects
* Steering defects
* Tire failures
* Critical warning lights
* Major fluid leaks
* Regulatory compliance concerns

## Immediate Actions

Management shall:

* Remove vehicle from service
* Notify affected personnel
* Coordinate repairs
* Document status

## Vehicle Status Tracking

Track:

* Tractor number
* Date removed from service
* Reason removed
* Repair vendor
* Estimated return date
* Actual return date

## Return to Service Requirements

Prior to returning a tractor to service:

* Repairs must be completed
* Safety concerns resolved
* Documentation retained
* Management approval obtained

## Documentation

Maintain:

* Inspection findings
* Repair records
* Return-to-service verification

## Objective

Prevent unsafe vehicles from operating while supporting rapid restoration of fleet capacity.
