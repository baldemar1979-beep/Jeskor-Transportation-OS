# DVIR Management Process

## Purpose

This document establishes Jeskor Transportation's Driver Vehicle Inspection Report management process.

The objective is to identify vehicle defects, maintain compliance, and support safe operations.

## Inspection Requirements

Drivers shall complete:

* Pre-trip inspections
* Post-trip inspections
* Defect reporting

## Inspection Focus Areas

Drivers should inspect:

* Brakes
* Tires
* Lights
* Steering
* Suspension
* Mirrors
* Windshield
* Fluid leaks
* Safety equipment

## Critical Defects

Examples:

* Brake issues
* Steering issues
* Tire failures
* Lighting deficiencies
* Air leaks

## Reporting Requirements

Drivers shall immediately report:

* Safety-related defects
* Out-of-service conditions
* Operational concerns

## Management Responsibilities

Management shall:

* Review DVIR submissions
* Track defect resolution
* Coordinate repairs
* Verify repair completion

## Documentation

Maintain:

* DVIR records
* Defect tracking logs
* Repair verification
* Vendor invoices

## Performance Metrics

Monitor:

* DVIR completion rate
* Open defects
* Repair turnaround time
* Repeat defects

## Objective

Identify and resolve defects before they impact safety, compliance, or operations.
