# Vendor Management Program

## Purpose

This document establishes procedures for selecting, evaluating, and managing fleet service vendors.

The objective is to maintain reliable vendor relationships that support tractor uptime and operational performance.

## Vendor Categories

* Leasing providers
* Mobile repair vendors
* Tire vendors
* Towing vendors
* Roadside assistance vendors
* Maintenance shops

## Vendor Selection Criteria

Evaluate:

* Commercial tractor experience
* Response time capability
* Geographic coverage
* Pricing structure
* Communication quality
* After-hours availability
* Service reputation

## Vendor Expectations

Preferred vendors should provide:

* Reliable response times
* Clear communication
* Accurate invoices
* Quality repairs
* Documentation support

## Vendor Performance Reviews

Review:

* Response time
* Repair quality
* Cost effectiveness
* Repeat repairs
* Driver feedback
* Management feedback

## Escalation Process

Vendor concerns shall be reviewed by management.

Repeated service failures may result in vendor removal from preferred status.

## Documentation

Maintain:

* Vendor contact information
* Service history
* Repair invoices
* Performance notes
* Escalation records

## Objective

Maintain reliable vendors that support safe and efficient tractor fleet operations.
