# Driver Onboarding Process

## Purpose

This document establishes the standard onboarding process for all newly hired drivers.

---

# Phase 1 - Pre-Arrival

Complete:

- Hiring approval
- Driver Qualification File
- Payroll setup
- Technology setup

---

# Phase 2 - Orientation

Review:

- Company overview
- AFP program overview
- Safety expectations
- Driver responsibilities

---

# Phase 3 - Compliance

Verify:

- CDL
- Medical card
- Drug testing
- Clearinghouse status

---

# Phase 4 - Technology

Train:

- Relay
- ELD
- Netradyne
- Electronic inspections

---

# Phase 5 - Safety

Review:

- Vehicle inspections
- Three points of contact
- Fatigue management
- Accident reporting
- Emergency procedures

---

# Phase 6 - Operational Readiness

Verify:

- Dispatch communication
- Route procedures
- Yard procedures
- Equipment handling

---

# Phase 7 - Driver Activation

Confirm:

☐ Training completed

☐ Technology access confirmed

☐ Safety review completed

☐ Compliance requirements completed

Driver approved for dispatch.
