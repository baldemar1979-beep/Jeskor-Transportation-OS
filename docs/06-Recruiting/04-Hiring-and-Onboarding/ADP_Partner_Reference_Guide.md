# ADP Partner Reference Guide

## Purpose

This document serves as the primary reference for ADP payroll, onboarding, employee administration, and workforce management activities at Jeskor Transportation.

ADP will be used as the company's Human Resources Information System (HRIS) and payroll platform for Amazon Freight Partner operations.

---

# System Overview

ADP provides the following capabilities:

- Payroll processing
- Employee onboarding
- Electronic I-9 completion
- E-Verify integration
- Direct deposit administration
- Employee records management
- Timekeeping
- Scheduling
- PTO management
- Compliance reporting
- Tax filing services

---

# Payroll Administration

ADP will support:

- Weekly payroll processing
- Direct deposit management
- Pay statement distribution
- Tax withholding calculations
- Wage garnishments
- W-2 generation
- Payroll reporting

### Jeskor Standard

All employees will be enrolled in direct deposit prior to their first payroll cycle.

---

# Employee Onboarding

ADP onboarding functionality supports:

- New hire information collection
- Electronic signatures
- Policy acknowledgements
- Tax forms
- Emergency contacts
- Direct deposit setup

### Required Prior To Day One

- Offer acceptance
- Driver qualification review
- Drug testing completion
- MVR review
- ADP profile creation

---

# I-9 and E-Verify

ADP assists with:

- Form I-9 completion
- Employment eligibility verification
- E-Verify integration
- Compliance documentation

### Jeskor Standard

All I-9 documentation must be completed prior to employee activation.

---

# Timekeeping
