# Ideal Driver Profile

## Purpose

This document defines the ideal driver candidate for Jeskor Transportation.

The objective is to establish a consistent hiring standard that aligns with company values, Amazon Freight Partner requirements, safety expectations, and long-term operational success.

---

# Hiring Philosophy

The ideal driver is not necessarily the most experienced driver.

The ideal driver is a safe, reliable, coachable professional who consistently makes good decisions.

---

# Core Characteristics

## Safety First

The driver:

- Prioritizes safety over speed
- Uses proper vehicle entry and exit techniques
- Performs thorough inspections
- Reports hazards immediately
- Accepts accountability for mistakes

---

## Reliability

The driver:

- Arrives on time
- Follows schedules
- Maintains attendance
- Communicates proactively
- Completes assigned work

---

## Professionalism

The driver:

- Treats others respectfully
- Represents Jeskor professionally
- Maintains a positive attitude
- Communicates clearly
- Maintains composure under pressure

---

## Coachability

The driver:

- Accepts feedback
- Learns from mistakes
- Embraces training
- Adapts to change
- Demonstrates humility

---

## Technology Acceptance

The driver:

- Uses mobile applications comfortably
- Understands ELD systems
- Accepts driver-facing safety technology
- Learns new systems quickly
- Follows electronic workflows

---

# Physical Expectations

The driver must be capable of:

- Entering and exiting tractors safely
- Maintaining three points of contact
- Conducting pre-trip inspections
- Walking around equipment
- Securing equipment properly
- Performing routine job functions safely

---

# Preferred Experience

Ideal:

- 2+ years CDL experience
- Clean MVR
- Positive safety history
- Stable employment history

Experience is preferred but does not outweigh safety mindset and character.

---

# Preferred Military Traits

Candidates demonstrating the following are highly valued:

- Accountability
- Discipline
- Leadership
- Teamwork
- Mission focus
- Adaptability

Military service is not required.

---

# Candidate Warning Signs

Additional review required for:

- Excessive job hopping
- Poor references
- Multiple preventable accidents
- Technology resistance
- Attendance concerns
- Blaming others for mistakes
- Safety shortcuts

---

# Jeskor Driver Statement

A Jeskor Driver is a safety-focused professional who consistently demonstrates accountability, reliability, coachability, and respect while embracing technology and representing the company with integrity.

---

# Hiring Principle

Never hire based solely on experience.

Prioritize:

1. Safety
2. Character
3. Reliability
4. Coachability
5. Technology Acceptance
6. Experience
