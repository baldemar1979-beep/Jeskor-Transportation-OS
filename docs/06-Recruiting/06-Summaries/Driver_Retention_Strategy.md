# Driver Retention Strategy

## Purpose

This document establishes Jeskor Transportation's driver retention strategy.

The objective is to retain safe, reliable, and productive drivers while reducing turnover costs, improving operational consistency, and strengthening company culture.

---

# Retention Philosophy

Recruiting fills seats.

Retention builds companies.

A retained driver provides:

- Lower recruiting costs
- Better safety performance
- Improved customer service
- Greater operational consistency
- Increased profitability

---

# Retention Pillars

## 1. Respect

Drivers will be treated with professionalism and respect.

Management will:

- Communicate honestly
- Address concerns promptly
- Maintain professional interactions
- Support driver success

---

## 2. Predictability

Drivers value consistency.

Provide:

- Predictable schedules
- Predictable home time
- Predictable expectations

---

## 3. Safety Culture

Drivers should never feel pressured to operate unsafely.

Management will:

- Support safe decisions
- Encourage hazard reporting
- Reward safety performance
- Promote accountability

---

## 4. Communication

Maintain open communication.

Topics include:

- Scheduling
- Performance
- Safety
- Driver concerns
- Operational changes

---

## 5. Recognition

Recognize positive performance.

Examples:

- Safe driving milestones
- Perfect attendance
- Customer compliments
- Team contributions

---

# Driver Engagement

Conduct periodic driver meetings.

Topics:

- Safety
- Operations
- Improvement opportunities
- Driver feedback

---

# Early Warning Indicators

Monitor:

- Attendance changes
- Increased complaints
- Policy violations
- Safety concerns
- Negative attitude changes
- Reduced communication

---

# Stay Interview Program

Conduct periodic stay interviews.

Questions:

- What do you enjoy most about working here?
- What frustrations are you experiencing?
- What would improve your experience?
- What would cause you to leave?

---

# Exit Interview Program

When drivers leave, document:

- Reason for departure
- Management concerns
- Pay concerns
- Schedule concerns
- Safety concerns

Track trends over time.

---

# Retention Metrics

Track:

- 30-Day Retention
- 90-Day Retention
- 180-Day Retention
- Annual Retention
- Voluntary Turnover
- Involuntary Turnover

---

# Long-Term Objective

Build a stable workforce of safe, professional drivers who view Jeskor Transportation as a long-term career opportunity rather than a temporary employer.
