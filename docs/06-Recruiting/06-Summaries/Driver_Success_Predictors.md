# Driver Success Predictors

## Purpose

This document identifies characteristics commonly associated with successful Jeskor Transportation drivers.

The objective is to improve hiring decisions by focusing on indicators that correlate with long-term success.

---

# Top Success Predictors

## Safety Mindset

Indicators:

- Discusses safety proactively
- Accepts accountability
- Uses proper procedures
- Reports hazards

---

## Stable Employment

Indicators:

- Consistent employment history
- Limited job hopping
- Positive references

---

## Technology Acceptance

Indicators:

- Comfortable with smartphones
- Comfortable with ELD systems
- Accepts camera technology

---

## Coachability

Indicators:

- Accepts feedback
- Demonstrates humility
- Learns from mistakes

---

## Communication

Indicators:

- Clear communication
- Professional interactions
- Timely responses

---

## Reliability

Indicators:

- Strong attendance history
- Consistent work habits
- Follows commitments

---

# High Performing Driver Traits

- Professional appearance
- Positive attitude
- Safety-focused decision making
- Accountability
- Situational awareness
- Adaptability

---

# Hiring Guidance

Prioritize:

1. Safety
2. Reliability
3. Coachability
4. Technology Acceptance
5. Communication
6. Experience

Experience alone should never outweigh safety and character.

---

# Continuous Improvement

Review annually.

Update based on:

- Safety performance
- Retention data
- Accident trends
- Operational feedback
