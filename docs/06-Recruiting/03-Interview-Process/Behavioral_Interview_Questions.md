# Behavioral Interview Questions

## Purpose

This document provides behavioral interview questions used to evaluate candidate character, professionalism, communication, and coachability.

---

# Questions

## Accountability

Tell me about a mistake you made and how you handled it.

---

## Teamwork

Describe a situation where you had to work with a difficult coworker.

---

## Communication

Tell me about a time you had to communicate bad news.

---

## Adaptability

Describe a situation where procedures changed unexpectedly.

---

## Conflict Resolution

Describe a disagreement with a supervisor.

---

## Professionalism

How would your previous manager describe you?

---

## Coachability

Tell me about a time you received difficult feedback.

---

# Evaluation Criteria

- Ownership
- Honesty
- Communication
- Humility
- Professionalism

---

# Scoring

1-10
