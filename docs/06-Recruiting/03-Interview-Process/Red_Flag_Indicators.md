# Red Flag Indicators

## Purpose

This document identifies warning signs that may indicate elevated hiring risk.

---

# Interview Red Flags

- Blames others for mistakes
- Refuses accountability
- Excessive negativity
- Poor communication
- Inconsistent responses

---

# Safety Red Flags

- Downplays safety incidents
- Admits taking shortcuts
- Minimizes risk
- Resists safety technology

---

# Employment Red Flags

- Frequent job hopping
- Unexplained employment gaps
- Multiple terminations
- Negative references

---

# Technology Red Flags

- Refuses technology
- Avoids learning new systems
- Negative reaction to Netradyne

---

# Professionalism Red Flags

- Arrives late
- Poor preparation
- Disrespectful behavior
- Unprofessional appearance

---

# Hiring Guidance

One red flag does not automatically disqualify a candidate.

Multiple red flags require management review before hire approval.
