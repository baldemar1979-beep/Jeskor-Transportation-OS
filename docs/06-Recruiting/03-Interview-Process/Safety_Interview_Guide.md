# Safety Interview Guide

## Purpose

This guide provides structured safety-focused interview questions used to evaluate driver candidates.

The objective is to identify candidates who consistently prioritize safety and demonstrate sound decision-making.

---

# Safety Questions

## Question 1

Tell me about a time you stopped work because something was unsafe.

Evaluate:

- Ownership
- Decision making
- Confidence

---

## Question 2

Describe a preventable mistake you made and what you learned.

Evaluate:

- Accountability
- Learning mindset

---

## Question 3

What would you do if dispatch instructed you to violate Hours of Service?

Evaluate:

- Integrity
- Safety commitment

---

## Question 4

Tell me about a close call you experienced.

Evaluate:

- Hazard recognition
- Situational awareness

---

## Question 5

How do you prepare yourself for long shifts?

Evaluate:

- Fatigue awareness
- Personal responsibility

---

# Scoring

1-10

1-3 = Poor

4-6 = Average

7-8 = Good

9-10 = Excellent
