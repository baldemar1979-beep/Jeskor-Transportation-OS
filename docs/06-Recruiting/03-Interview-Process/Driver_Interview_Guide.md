# Driver Interview Guide

## Purpose

This document establishes the standard interview process used by Jeskor Transportation to evaluate driver candidates.

The objective is to identify candidates who demonstrate strong safety habits, professionalism, communication skills, and operational readiness.

---

# Interview Structure

Interview Duration:

30-45 Minutes

Interview Type:

Structured Interview

Scoring Method:

Standardized Evaluation

---

# Section 1 - Introduction

Explain:

- Company overview
- Amazon Freight Partner program
- Schedule expectations
- Safety expectations
- Technology expectations

---

# Section 2 - Employment History

Questions:

### Tell me about your driving experience.

### What type of equipment have you operated?

### Why are you leaving your current employer?

### Describe your ideal work environment.

Evaluate:

- Stability
- Professionalism
- Communication

Score: 1-10

---

# Section 3 - Safety Assessment

Questions:

### Tell me about a time you stopped work because something was unsafe.

### Tell me about a mistake you made and what you learned from it.

### Describe a close call you experienced.

### What does a safe driver look like?

Evaluate:

- Ownership
- Accountability
- Safety mindset

Score: 1-10

---

# Section 4 - Technology Assessment

Questions:

### What ELD systems have you used?

### Have you worked with driver-facing cameras?

### Have you used electronic DVIR systems?

### Are you comfortable learning new technology?

Evaluate:

- Adaptability
- Technology comfort
- Coachability

Score: 1-10

---

# Section 5 - Customer Service Assessment

Questions:

### Describe a difficult interaction with a customer or dispatcher.

### How do you handle conflicting priorities?

### How would previous supervisors describe you?

Evaluate:

- Professionalism
- Communication
- Conflict resolution

Score: 1-10

---

# Section 6 - AFP Specific Questions

### Can you work nights?

### Can you work weekends?

### Can you work during peak periods?

### Are you comfortable operating under strict safety standards?

### Are you comfortable being monitored by safety technology?

Evaluate:

- Flexibility
- AFP readiness
- Acceptance of technology

Score: 1-10
