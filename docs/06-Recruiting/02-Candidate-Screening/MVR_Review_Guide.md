# MVR Review Guide

## Purpose

This document establishes the standard process for reviewing Motor Vehicle Records (MVRs) for driver candidates.

---

# Automatic Disqualifiers

Candidates will not be considered if any of the following are identified:

- Suspended CDL
- Revoked CDL
- DUI/DWI within 5 years
- Reckless driving conviction within 5 years
- Hit and run conviction
- License fraud
- Falsified application

---

# Major Violations

Review Required:

- Excessive speeding
- Following too closely
- Improper lane changes
- Serious traffic offenses

---

# Accident Review

Evaluate:

- Preventable accidents
- Non-preventable accidents
- Injury accidents
- Frequency of accidents

---

# Point System

## Clean MVR

+25

## No Violations Within 3 Years

+20

## Minor Violation

-5

## Major Violation

-15

## Preventable Accident

-10

---

# Hiring Guidance

90-100 = Preferred Candidate

80-89 = Acceptable Candidate

70-79 = Management Review

Below 70 = Do Not Hire

---

# Documentation

All MVR reviews must be documented and retained in the candidate file.
