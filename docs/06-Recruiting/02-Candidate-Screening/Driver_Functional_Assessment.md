# Driver Functional Assessment

## Purpose

The Driver Functional Assessment is designed to evaluate candidate behaviors that may not be visible during a traditional interview.

The assessment focuses on safety habits, physical capability, professionalism, situational awareness, and technology readiness.

## Assessment Areas

### 1. Arrival Observation

Observe:

- Arrives early
- Arrives on time
- Arrives late
- Appearance
- Personal hygiene
- Professional attitude

### 2. Parking Observation

Observe:

- Parks appropriately
- Uses mirrors effectively
- Demonstrates awareness of surroundings
- Avoids distracted behavior while parking

Score: 1-5

### 3. Walking Assessment

Observe:

- Walking speed
- Mobility
- Balance
- Ability to navigate uneven surfaces
- General physical condition

### 4. Cab Entry and Exit

Observe:

- Uses three points of contact
- Maintains awareness while entering
- Maintains awareness while exiting
- Handles personal items safely

Score: 1-5

### 5. Vehicle Inspection Walkthrough

Ask:

"Walk me through how you would conduct a pre-trip inspection."

Observe:

- Confidence
- Organization
- Safety awareness
- Inspection knowledge
- Communication ability

Score: 1-10

### 6. Safety Mindset

Ask:

"Tell me about a time you stopped work because something was unsafe."

Evaluate:

- Ownership
- Judgment
- Decision making
- Accountability

Score: 1-10

### 7. Technology Assessment

Ask:

- Experience with ELD systems
- Experience with driver-facing cameras
- Experience with mobile applications
- Experience with electronic inspections

Score: 1-10

### 8. Communication Assessment

Evaluate:

- Listening skills
- Ability to follow instructions
- Professionalism
- Ability to explain problems

Score: 1-10

## Overall Recommendation

### Hire

Candidate demonstrates strong safety habits, professionalism, and operational readiness.

### Consider

Candidate demonstrates potential but requires coaching.

### Do Not Hire

Candidate demonstrates behaviors inconsistent with company safety standards or operational expectations.
