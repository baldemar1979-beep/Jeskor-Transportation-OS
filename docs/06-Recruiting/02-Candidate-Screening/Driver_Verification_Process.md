# Driver Verification Process

## Purpose

This document establishes the standard verification process for all driver candidates prior to hire.

The objective is to verify that candidates meet Jeskor Transportation standards, Amazon Freight Partner requirements, and all applicable regulatory requirements.

---

# Verification Requirements

A candidate must successfully complete all verification steps before receiving a final employment offer.

---

## Step 1 - CDL Verification

Verify:

- Valid CDL
- Correct CDL class
- State of issuance
- Expiration date
- Required endorsements

### Pass Criteria

- CDL is active
- No pending suspension
- No restrictions that prevent job performance

---

## Step 2 - Motor Vehicle Record (MVR) Review

Review:

- Traffic violations
- Moving violations
- License suspensions
- Reckless driving offenses
- DUI / DWI offenses

### Automatic Disqualifiers

- Suspended CDL
- Revoked CDL
- Current DUI restrictions
- Falsified driving history

### Review Required

- Multiple speeding violations
- At-fault accidents
- Serious traffic offenses

---

## Step 3 - PSP Review

Review:

- FMCSA roadside inspections
- Out-of-service violations
- Driver violations
- Safety history

### Evaluate

- Frequency
- Severity
- Corrective actions

---

## Step 4 - Employment Verification

Verify:

- Previous employers
- Dates of employment
- Commercial driving experience
- Separation reason

### Confirm

- Employment history matches application
- Experience level claimed by candidate

---

## Step 5 - Accident History Review

Review:

- Preventable accidents
- Non-preventable accidents
- Severity of accidents
- Injury involvement

### Evaluate

- Frequency
- Accountability
- Lessons learned

---

## Step 6 - Drug and Alcohol Verification

Verify:

- Drug testing eligibility
- Clearinghouse registration
- Clearinghouse query results

### Requirements

- Negative drug test
- No unresolved Clearinghouse violations

---

## Step 7 - Reference Checks

Minimum:

- Two professional references

Evaluate:

- Reliability
- Attendance
- Safety behavior
- Professionalism
- Communication
