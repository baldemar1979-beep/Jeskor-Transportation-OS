# Fatigue Risk Assessment

## Purpose

This document identifies fatigue-related risk factors that may impact driver safety and long-term performance.

The objective is to identify candidates who may require additional review, coaching, or medical evaluation.

---

# Candidate Interview Questions

## Sleep

How many hours do you typically sleep each night?

Do you feel rested when you wake up?

Do you nap frequently?

---

## Work History

Have you previously worked night shifts?

Have you previously worked rotating schedules?

How do you manage fatigue?

---

## Commute

How far is your commute?

How long does it take to travel to work?

---

## Secondary Employment

Do you currently work another job?

Do you plan to maintain secondary employment?

---

# Fatigue Risk Indicators

Review:

- Long commute
- Multiple jobs
- Poor sleep habits
- Frequent daytime fatigue
- Previous fatigue-related incidents
- Chronic sleep deprivation

---

# Additional Review Indicators

Candidate reports:

- Falling asleep unintentionally
- Difficulty staying awake while driving
- Excessive daytime sleepiness

Escalate for additional review.
