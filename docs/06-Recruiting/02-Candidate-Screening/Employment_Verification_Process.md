# Employment Verification Process

## Purpose

This document establishes the standard process for verifying employment history for driver candidates.

The objective is to validate candidate experience, identify inconsistencies, and reduce hiring risk.

---

# Verification Requirements

Verify all CDL-related employment for the previous 3 years.

Preferred:

Verify previous 5 years.

---

# Information To Verify

Confirm:

- Employer name
- Employment dates
- Position held
- Type of equipment operated
- Reason for separation
- Eligibility for rehire

---

# Employment Gap Review

Identify:

- Employment gaps greater than 30 days
- Unexplained periods of unemployment
- Inconsistent employment dates

Candidate must provide explanations.

---

# Rehire Eligibility

Ask:

Would you rehire this employee?

Responses:

- Yes
- No
- Eligible with conditions

Document explanation.

---

# Performance Review

Evaluate:

- Attendance
- Reliability
- Professionalism
- Safety performance
- Policy compliance

---

# Red Flags

Additional review required for:

- Frequent job changes
- Unexplained gaps
- Safety concerns
- Attendance concerns
- Poor supervisor feedback
- Inconsistent application information

---

# Documentation

Maintain:

- Employer name
- Contact information
- Date verified
- Notes
- Reviewer

Store in candidate file.
