# Fountain Workflow Guide

## Purpose

This document establishes the standard recruiting workflow used within Fountain.

The objective is to ensure all candidates move through a consistent and documented recruiting process.

---

# Candidate Workflow

Application Submitted

↓

Application Review

↓

Phone Screening

↓

Driver Verification

↓

Interview

↓

Conditional Offer

↓

Drug Test

↓

Background Review

↓

Hiring Approval

↓

Onboarding

↓

Active Driver

---

# Application Review

Evaluate:

- Experience
- CDL status
- Location
- Work history

Decision:

- Advance
- Hold
- Reject

---

# Phone Screening

Verify:

- Interest level
- Schedule compatibility
- Compensation expectations
- Availability

---

# Driver Verification

Complete:

- MVR review
- PSP review
- Employment verification
- Reference checks

---

# Interview

Complete:

- Functional assessment
- Safety interview
- Behavioral interview
- Technology assessment

---

# Hiring Approval

Required Approvals:

- Recruiting
- Operations
- Safety

---

# Onboarding

Complete:

- Drug testing
- DQ file
- Payroll setup
- Technology setup

---

# Driver Activation

Confirm:

- Compliance complete
- Training complete
- Technology active

Driver approved for dispatch.

Review workflow effectiveness and identify bottlenecks.
