# Fountain Best Practices

## Purpose

This document captures lessons learned and best practices for operating Fountain effectively.

---

# Candidate Response Time

Target:

Contact all qualified candidates within 24 hours.

---

# Interview Scheduling

Target:

Schedule interviews within 72 hours.

---

# Candidate Communication

Maintain communication throughout the recruiting process.

Avoid candidate abandonment.

---

# Status Management

Update candidate statuses promptly.

Avoid stale candidate records.

---

# Documentation

Document:

- Interview notes
- Verification findings
- Hiring decisions

Maintain complete records.

---

# Reporting

Review recruiting metrics monthly.

Identify trends and bottlenecks.

---

# Continuous Improvement

Capture lessons learned.

Update workflows as recruiting processes mature.
