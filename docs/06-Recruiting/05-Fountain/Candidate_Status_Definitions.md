# Candidate Status Definitions

## Purpose

This document defines all candidate statuses used within Fountain.

The objective is to ensure consistent candidate tracking and reporting.

---

# Status Definitions

## Applied

Candidate submitted application.

---

## Application Review

Application under review.

---

## Phone Screen Scheduled

Phone interview scheduled.

---

## Phone Screen Complete

Phone interview completed.

---

## Verification In Progress

MVR, PSP, and employment verification underway.

---

## Interview Scheduled

Formal interview scheduled.

---

## Interview Complete

Interview completed.

---

## Conditional Offer

Candidate approved pending final requirements.

---

## Drug Test Pending

Drug test scheduled.

---

## Background Review

Background investigation in progress.

---

## Approved for Hire

Candidate approved.

---

## Onboarding

Candidate completing onboarding requirements.

---

## Active Driver

Driver activated.

---

## Rejected

Candidate removed from consideration.

---

## Candidate Withdrawn

Candidate voluntarily withdrew.

---

# Status Management

All status changes must occur within 24 hours of candidate progression.
