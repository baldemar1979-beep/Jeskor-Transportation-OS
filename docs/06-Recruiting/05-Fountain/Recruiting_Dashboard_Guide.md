# Recruiting Dashboard Guide

## Purpose

This document defines recruiting performance reporting standards.

The objective is to provide visibility into recruiting effectiveness and hiring outcomes.

---

# Dashboard Metrics

## Applications Received

Monthly applications received.

---

## Qualified Candidates

Candidates meeting minimum qualifications.

---

## Interviews Completed

Candidates completing interviews.

---

## Offers Extended

Offers made.

---

## Hires

Candidates hired.

---

## 30-Day Retention

Drivers remaining employed after 30 days.

---

## 90-Day Retention

Drivers remaining employed after 90 days.

---

## Cost Per Hire

Total recruiting cost divided by total hires.

---

## Source Performance

Track:

- Indeed
- Referrals
- Military Recruiting
- CDL Schools

---

# Monthly Review

Review recruiting performance monthly.

Identify:

- Bottlenecks
- Source effectiveness
- Retention concerns
- Quality concerns

Implement corrective actions.
