# Indeed Recruiting Strategy

## Purpose

This document establishes Jeskor Transportation's recruiting strategy using Indeed as the primary candidate sourcing platform.

The objective is to create a sustainable recruiting pipeline capable of supplying qualified drivers for current and future Amazon Freight Partner operations.

---

# Recruiting Philosophy

Jeskor Transportation may not always offer the highest hourly wage in the market.

Instead, Jeskor will compete by providing:

- Stable schedules
- Predictable home time
- Professional management
- Safety-focused culture
- Modern technology
- Respectful treatment of drivers
- Career development opportunities

---

# Primary Recruiting Sources

## Indeed

Primary candidate sourcing platform.

Target:

- Local CDL drivers
- Regional CDL drivers
- Drivers seeking predictable schedules
- Drivers leaving high-turnover carriers

---

## Employee Referrals

Encourage current employees to recommend qualified candidates.

Benefits:

- Higher retention
- Faster onboarding
- Improved culture fit

---

## Military Recruiting

Target:

- Veterans
- National Guard
- Reserve personnel

Benefits:

- Safety mindset
- Discipline
- Accountability
- Adaptability

---

## CDL Schools

Develop relationships with:

- Local CDL schools
- Community colleges
- Driver training programs

Purpose:

Create long-term recruiting pipelines.

---

## Social Media Recruiting

Platforms:

- Facebook
- CDL Driver Groups
- Community Groups

Purpose:

Increase local candidate visibility.

---

# Indeed Job Posting Standards

All job postings should clearly communicate:

- Pay structure
- Schedule
- Home time
- Benefits
- Safety culture
- Technology expectations

Avoid misleading language.

---

# Candidate Response Standards

Initial candidate contact goal:

Within 24 hours.

Interview scheduling goal:

Within 72 hours.

Application review goal:

Within 48 hours.

---

# Recruiting Performance Metrics

Track:

- Applications received
- Qualified candidates
- Interview rate
- Offer rate
- Hire rate
- Cost per hire
- 90-day retention
- 180-day retention

---

# Continuous Improvement

Review recruiting performance monthly.

Identify:

- Strong recruiting channels
- Weak recruiting channels
- Candidate quality trends
- Hiring bottlenecks

Implement improvements as needed.
