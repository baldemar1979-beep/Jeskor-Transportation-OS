# Employee Referral Program

## Purpose

This document establishes Jeskor Transportation's Employee Referral Program.

The objective is to attract qualified candidates through trusted employee recommendations while improving hiring quality, retention, and recruiting efficiency.

---

# Program Philosophy

Employees who enjoy working at Jeskor Transportation are often the best source of future employees.

Referred candidates frequently demonstrate:

- Better cultural fit
- Higher retention rates
- Faster onboarding success
- Stronger safety performance

---

# Eligible Referrals

Current employees may refer:

- CDL Drivers
- Dispatch Personnel
- Administrative Personnel
- Fleet Personnel
- Safety Personnel

---

# Referral Requirements

The referred candidate must:

- Complete the hiring process
- Meet all hiring standards
- Pass required screenings
- Successfully complete onboarding

Referral submissions must be made before the candidate applies.

---

# Referral Submission Process

Step 1

Employee submits referral information.

Required:

- Candidate name
- Phone number
- Email address
- Position referred for

---

Step 2

Recruiting contacts candidate.

---

Step 3

Candidate completes recruiting process.

---

Step 4

Referral eligibility verified.

---

# Referral Bonus Guidelines

Referral bonus amounts may be adjusted by management based on business needs.

Examples:

### CDL Driver

$500-$2,000

### Administrative Roles

$250-$500

### Specialized Roles

Management Discretion

---

# Bonus Payment Requirements

Candidate must:

- Complete onboarding
- Meet performance expectations
- Remain employed through required retention period

Recommended retention period:

90 Days

---

# Disqualification Conditions

Referral bonus may be denied if:

- Candidate provided false information
- Candidate is terminated for misconduct
- Referral was submitted after application
- Hiring standards were bypassed

---

# Recruiting Metrics

Track:

- Referral applications
- Referral interviews
- Referral hires
- Referral retention
- Referral turnover

---

# Program Review

Review quarterly:

- Referral participation
- Referral quality
- Retention performance
- Bonus effectiveness

Adjust program as necessary.

---

# Long-Term Objective

Develop employee referrals into a primary recruiting source that produces high-quality candidates, improves retention, and strengthens company culture.
