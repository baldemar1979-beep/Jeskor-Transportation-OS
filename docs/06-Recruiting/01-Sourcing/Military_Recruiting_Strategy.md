# Military Recruiting Strategy

## Purpose

This document establishes Jeskor Transportation's military recruiting strategy.

The objective is to leverage military hiring programs, veteran networks, and military leadership experience to attract high-quality driver candidates.

---

# Recruiting Philosophy

Military service often develops characteristics that align closely with successful transportation professionals.

Desired characteristics include:

- Accountability
- Discipline
- Safety awareness
- Adaptability
- Leadership
- Reliability
- Teamwork

Jeskor Transportation actively seeks candidates who demonstrate these characteristics.

---

# Target Candidate Groups

## Military Veterans

Target:

- Army
- Air Force
- Navy
- Marines
- Coast Guard
- Space Force

---

## National Guard

Target currently serving National Guard members seeking civilian employment.

---

## Military Reserves

Target currently serving Reserve members seeking civilian employment.

---

## Military Family Members

Target military spouses and family members for administrative and support positions.

---

# Recruiting Channels

## Hiring Our Heroes

Utilize veteran employment resources and networking opportunities.

---

## RecruitMilitary

Participate in military recruiting events and candidate databases.

---

## Military Facebook Groups

Target:

- Veteran groups
- Local military communities
- Transition assistance groups

---

## Employee Referrals

Encourage veteran employees to recommend qualified candidates.

---

# Military Interview Focus Areas

Evaluate:

- Leadership experience
- Accountability
- Safety mindset
- Ability to follow procedures
- Adaptability to changing environments
- Team-oriented behavior

- Predictable schedules
- Professional leadership
- Growth opportunities
- Recognition programs
- Safety-focused environment

---

- Veteran applications
- Veteran interviews
- Veteran hires

# Long-Term Objective

Build a workforce that leverages veteran leadership, accountability, and safety-focused decision making while creating employment opportunities for military members transitioning into civilian careers.
