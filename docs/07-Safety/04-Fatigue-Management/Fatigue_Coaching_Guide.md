# Fatigue Coaching Guide

## Purpose

This guide provides standardized coaching techniques for fatigue-related events.

---

# Coaching Philosophy

The objective is to understand contributing factors and reduce future fatigue risk.

Fatigue discussions should focus on support and education.

---

# Coaching Questions

### Sleep

How many hours of sleep are you averaging?

Do you wake feeling rested?

---

### Schedule

How are you adjusting to your schedule?

Any recent changes?

---

### Commute

How long is your commute?

Has it impacted your rest?

---

### Secondary Employment

Are you working additional jobs?

---

### Health

Any health concerns impacting sleep quality?

---

# Root Cause Categories

- Sleep deprivation
- Schedule adjustment
- Commute
- Lifestyle
- Health concerns
- Secondary employment

---

# Follow-Up Actions

Examples:

- Education
- Monitoring
- Schedule review
- Additional intervention
