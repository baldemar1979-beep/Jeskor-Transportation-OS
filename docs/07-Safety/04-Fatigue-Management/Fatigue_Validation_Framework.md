# Fatigue Validation Framework

## Purpose

This framework establishes the methodology used to determine whether a fatigue event is valid.

---

# Review Objectives

Determine:

- Is fatigue present?
- What severity exists?
- What evidence supports the determination?

---

# Critical Indicators

## Microsleep

Highest severity.

Examples:

- Eyes closed
- Loss of awareness
- Head drop

---

## Eyes Closed

Critical fatigue indicator.

---

# Supporting Indicators

Examples:

- Head bobbing
- Lane departure
- Delayed reactions
- Repeated yawning
- Extended blink duration

---

# Fatigue Classifications

## Not Fatigued

Criteria not met.

---

## Mild Fatigue

Early fatigue indicators present.

---

## Moderate Fatigue

Multiple indicators present.

---

## Severe Fatigue

High confidence fatigue event.

---

## Critical Fatigue

Microsleep or equivalent event.

---

# Documentation

Document:

- Indicators observed
- Severity
- Evidence
- Reviewer

---

# Continuous Improvement

Review classifications periodically to improve accuracy and consistency.
