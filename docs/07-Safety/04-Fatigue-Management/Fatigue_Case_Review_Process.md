# Fatigue Case Review Process

## Purpose

This document establishes the review process for fatigue-related events.

---

# Review Objectives

Determine:

- Fatigue validity
- Severity
- Contributing factors
- Appropriate interventions

---

# Case Review Process

## Step 1

Review fatigue evidence.

Examples:

- Video
- Driver statements
- Operational information

---

## Step 2

Classify Severity

- Low
- Moderate
- High
- Critical

---

## Step 3

Identify Contributing Factors

Examples:

- Sleep deprivation
- Schedule
- Long commute
- Secondary employment
- Health concerns

---

## Step 4

Determine Intervention

Examples:

- Coaching
- Education
- Escalation
- Additional review

---

## Step 5

Document Findings

Maintain:

- Case summary
- Severity
- Actions taken

---

# Trend Analysis

Review:

- Repeat drivers
- Repeat locations
- Repeat schedules
- Systemic issues
