# Preventability Review Process

## Purpose

This document establishes the standard process for determining accident preventability.

---

# Objective

Determine whether the driver could have reasonably prevented the incident.

---

# Review Team

Participants:

- Safety Manager
- Operations Representative
- Driver Representative (optional)

---

# Evidence Review

Review:

- Driver statement
- Witness statements
- Netradyne footage
- Police reports
- Vehicle data

---

# Determinations

## Preventable

Driver actions contributed to the incident.

---

## Non-Preventable

Driver could not reasonably avoid the incident.

---

## Undetermined

Insufficient information.

---

# Root Cause Categories

- Distraction
- Fatigue
- Speed
- Following Distance
- Decision Making
- Environmental Factors

---

# Documentation

Document:

- Incident Summary
- Determination
- Root Cause
- Corrective Actions

Maintain records in incident file.
