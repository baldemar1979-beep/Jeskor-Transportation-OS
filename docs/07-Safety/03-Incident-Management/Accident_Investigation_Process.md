# Accident Investigation Process

## Purpose

This document establishes the standard process for investigating accidents and incidents.

---

# Investigation Objectives

Determine:

- What happened
- Why it happened
- How to prevent recurrence

---

# Immediate Response

Collect:

- Driver statement
- Photographs
- Witness information
- Vehicle information

---

# Investigation Process

Step 1

Gather evidence.

Step 2

Interview driver.

Step 3

Review technology data.

Step 4

Determine root cause.

Step 5

Assign preventability.

Step 6

Identify corrective actions.

---

# Root Cause Categories

- Driver behavior
- Fatigue
- Distraction
- Vehicle issue
- Environmental factors
- Training deficiency

---

# Corrective Actions

Examples:

- Coaching
- Retraining
- Process changes
- Equipment changes

---

# Documentation

Maintain investigation records for all incidents.
