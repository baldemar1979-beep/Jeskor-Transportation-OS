# Safety Review Board Process

## Purpose

This document establishes the process for reviewing serious safety events.

---

# Review Objectives

Determine:

- Root cause
- Preventability
- Corrective actions
- Future risk mitigation

---

# Review Board Members

- Safety Manager
- Operations Manager
- Ownership Representative

Optional:

- Driver Representative

---

# Review Triggers

Examples:

- Serious accident
- Repeat fatigue events
- Repeat severe distraction
- Critical safety violation

---

# Review Process

Step 1

Gather evidence.

Step 2

Review event.

Step 3

Determine findings.

Step 4

Assign corrective actions.

Step 5

Document outcomes.

---

# Outcomes

- Coaching
- Retraining
- Monitoring
- Corrective action
- Policy changes

---

# Documentation

Maintain meeting notes and final decisions.
