# Serious Accident Response Plan

## Purpose

This document establishes the response process for serious accidents involving company drivers.

---

# Objectives

- Protect life
- Preserve evidence
- Support investigations
- Manage communications
- Reduce future risk

---

# Immediate Driver Actions

Driver shall:

- Ensure personal safety
- Contact emergency services
- Notify dispatch
- Secure the scene
- Avoid admitting fault

---

# Company Response

## Operations

- Confirm driver status
- Confirm scene status
- Support emergency response

---

## Safety

- Initiate investigation
- Collect evidence
- Coordinate reporting

---

# Evidence Collection

Collect:

- Photos
- Video
- Witness information
- Police report information
- Vehicle information

---

# Internal Notifications

Notify:

- Operations
- Safety
- Ownership
- Insurance Provider

---

# Investigation

Determine:

- Facts
- Preventability
- Root causes
- Corrective actions

---

# Post-Incident Review

Review:

- Lessons learned
- Policy improvements
- Training opportunities
