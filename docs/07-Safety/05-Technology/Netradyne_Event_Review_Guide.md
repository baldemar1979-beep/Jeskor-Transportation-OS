# Netradyne Event Review Guide

## Purpose

This document establishes the standard review process for Netradyne-generated safety events.

The objective is to ensure consistent event validation, coaching decisions, and performance tracking.

---

# Review Philosophy

Review the behavior, not the score.

The objective is to understand risk and improve driver performance.

---

# Event Review Process

## Step 1

Review event video.

Observe:

- Driver behavior
- Road conditions
- Traffic conditions
- Environmental factors

---

## Step 2

Validate Event

Determine:

- Valid
- Invalid
- System Error

---

## Step 3

Determine Severity

Low

Moderate

High

Critical

---

## Step 4

Identify Root Cause

Examples:

- Distraction
- Following distance
- Fatigue
- Decision making
- Speed management

---

## Step 5

Determine Action

Options:

- No Action
- Coaching
- Retraining
- Escalation

---

# Priority Review Categories

Highest Priority:

- Fatigue
- Severe Distraction
- Red Light Violations
- Collision Warnings

---

# Documentation

Record:

- Driver
- Event
- Root Cause
- Severity
- Corrective Action

---

# Program Objective

Reduce risk through consistent coaching and behavioral improvement.
