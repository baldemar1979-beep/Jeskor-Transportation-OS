# Driver Coaching Framework

## Purpose

This document establishes the standard driver coaching process.

The objective is to improve safety performance through consistent coaching and behavioral improvement.

---

# Coaching Philosophy

The goal is improvement, not punishment.

Coaching should:

- Identify risk
- Explain impact
- Gain commitment
- Measure improvement

---

# Coaching Levels

## Level 1

Informal Coaching

Examples:

- Minor speeding
- Following distance concerns
- Single distraction event

---

## Level 2

Formal Coaching

Examples:

- Repeated behaviors
- Significant risk events

---

## Level 3

Corrective Action Review

Examples:

- Severe events
- Failure to improve

---

# Coaching Process

Step 1

Review event.

Step 2

Discuss event with driver.

Step 3

Identify root cause.

Step 4

Agree on corrective action.

Step 5

Document coaching.

Step 6

Follow up.

---

# Documentation Requirements

Document:

- Driver
- Event
- Coaching discussion
- Commitments made
- Follow-up date
