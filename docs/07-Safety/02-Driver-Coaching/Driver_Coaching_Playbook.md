# Driver Coaching Playbook

## Purpose

This playbook provides standardized coaching guidance for supervisors and safety personnel.

The objective is to improve driver performance through effective coaching conversations.

---

# Coaching Principles

Effective coaching:

- Focuses on behavior
- Focuses on improvement
- Uses facts
- Avoids assumptions
- Builds trust

---

# Coaching Process

## Step 1

Review the event.

Gather:

- Event details
- Video evidence
- Driver history

---

## Step 2

Ask Questions

Examples:

- What happened?
- What were you seeing?
- What factors influenced your decision?

---

## Step 3

Identify Root Cause

Examples:

- Distraction
- Fatigue
- Poor judgment
- Knowledge gap
- Habit

---

## Step 4

Develop Corrective Actions

Examples:

- Additional training
- Follow-up coaching
- Observation period

---

## Step 5

Gain Commitment

Ask:

What will you do differently next time?

---

# Coaching Documentation

Document:

- Event
- Discussion
- Root cause
- Action plan
- Follow-up date

---

# Coaching Success Indicators

- Reduced repeat events
- Improved safety scores
- Positive driver engagement
- Strong follow-through
