# Amazon Safety Enforcement Guide

## Purpose

This document summarizes Amazon Freight Partner safety enforcement expectations and associated operational risks.

The objective is to identify behaviors and program failures that may result in enforcement actions.

---

# Safety Philosophy

Amazon evaluates both:

- Individual events
- Organizational behavior

Failure to address recurring safety issues increases enforcement risk.

---

# High Risk Safety Categories

## Preventable Crashes

Monitor:

- Frequency
- Severity
- Root causes
- Corrective actions

---

## Failure to Report Incidents

Examples:

- Delayed reporting
- Missing documentation
- Incomplete investigations

---

## Safety Technology Tampering

Examples:

- Netradyne tampering
- Device obstruction
- Unauthorized modifications

---

## Safety Training Deficiencies

Examples:

- Missed training
- Incomplete certifications
- Expired qualifications

---

## Investigation Cooperation

Drivers and management shall fully cooperate with investigations.

---

# Escalation Indicators

Examples:

- Repeat preventable crashes
- Repeat safety violations
- Failure to complete corrective actions
- Pattern of safety deficiencies

---

# Management Responsibilities

Review:

- Safety trends
- Coaching completion
- Enforcement exposure
- Corrective action status

---

# Objective

Reduce enforcement exposure by proactively managing safety performance and compliance.
