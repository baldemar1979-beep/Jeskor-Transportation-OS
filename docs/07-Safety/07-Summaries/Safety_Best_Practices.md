# Safety Best Practices

## Purpose

This document captures proven safety practices used by Jeskor Transportation.

---

# Core Principles

- Safety before productivity
- Coach before discipline
- Investigate before conclusions
- Measure what matters
- Continuously improve

---

# Fatigue Management

Best Practices:

- Early intervention
- Consistent validation
- Data-driven reviews
- Driver education

---

# Coaching

Best Practices:

- Focus on behavior
- Use evidence
- Gain commitment
- Follow up

---

# Technology

Best Practices:

- Validate events
- Review trends
- Focus on risk reduction
- Avoid score chasing

---

# Incident Management

Best Practices:

- Preserve evidence
- Determine root causes
- Share lessons learned

---

# Continuous Improvement

Review this document annually and update with lessons learned from operations.
