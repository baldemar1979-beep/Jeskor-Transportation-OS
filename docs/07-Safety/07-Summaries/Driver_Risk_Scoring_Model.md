# Driver Risk Scoring Model

## Purpose

This document establishes a driver risk scoring methodology used to identify elevated safety risk.

---

# Risk Categories

## Fatigue

Weight: 30%

---

## Distraction

Weight: 25%

---

## Preventable Accidents

Weight: 20%

---

## Coaching History

Weight: 15%

---

## Policy Compliance

Weight: 10%

---

# Risk Levels

## Low Risk

Score: 0-24

---

## Moderate Risk

Score: 25-49

---

## High Risk

Score: 50-74

---

## Critical Risk

Score: 75+

---

# Management Actions

Low Risk

Monitor

---

Moderate Risk

Coaching

---

High Risk

Formal Review

---

Critical Risk

Management Escalation

---

# Review Frequency

Monthly

---

# Objective

Identify drivers requiring proactive intervention before incidents occur.
