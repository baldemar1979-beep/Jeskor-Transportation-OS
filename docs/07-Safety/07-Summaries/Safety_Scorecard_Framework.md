# Safety Scorecard Framework

## Purpose

This document establishes the safety scorecard used to evaluate driver and fleet safety performance.

---

# Objectives

The scorecard is designed to:

- Measure safety performance
- Identify risk trends
- Support coaching efforts
- Improve accountability

---

# Driver Metrics

## Preventable Accidents

Target:

Zero

---

## Netradyne Events

Track:

- Distraction
- Following Distance
- Speeding
- Stop Sign
- Traffic Signal

---

## Fatigue Events

Track:

- Moderate Fatigue
- Severe Fatigue
- Microsleep Indicators

---

## Coaching Compliance

Track:

- Coaching Completion
- Follow-Up Completion

---

# Fleet Metrics

## Preventable Accident Rate

Track monthly.

---

## Fatigue Event Rate

Track monthly.

95%+

---

## Safety Technology Utilization

Target:

100%
