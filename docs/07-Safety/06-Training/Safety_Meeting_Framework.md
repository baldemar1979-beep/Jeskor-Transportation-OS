# Safety Meeting Framework

## Purpose

This document establishes a standard structure for recurring driver safety meetings.

---

# Objectives

- Reinforce safety culture
- Review trends
- Share lessons learned
- Improve driver engagement

---

# Meeting Agenda

## Opening

- Welcome
- Safety message

---

## Safety Metrics Review

Review:

- Accident trends
- Netradyne trends
- Fatigue trends
- Coaching completion

---

## Lessons Learned

Discuss:

- Recent incidents
- Near misses
- Industry events

---

## Training Topic

Examples:

- Fatigue
- Distracted driving
- Backing
- Defensive driving

---

## Driver Feedback

Allow:

- Questions
- Concerns
- Improvement suggestions

---

## Action Items

Document:

- Assignments
- Follow-up items
- Due dates

---

# Meeting Frequency

Recommended:

Monthly

---

# Documentation

Maintain:

- Attendance
- Agenda
- Training topic
- Action items
