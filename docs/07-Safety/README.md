# Safety Department

## Purpose

The Safety Department serves as the central repository for all safety-related policies, procedures, standards, reporting requirements, investigations, coaching programs, and performance management activities.

The objective is to create a proactive safety culture that protects drivers, the public, company assets, and Amazon Freight Partner operations.

---

# Department Structure

## 01-Safety-Policies

Company safety policies and expectations.

## 02-Driver-Coaching

Driver coaching procedures and corrective action programs.

## 03-Incident-Management

Accident investigations, reporting requirements, and corrective actions.

## 04-Fatigue-Management

Fatigue detection, intervention, and prevention programs.

## 05-Technology

Netradyne, Seeing Machines, ELD, and safety technology management.

## 06-Training

Safety training programs and development materials.

## 07-Summaries

Executive summaries, lessons learned, and best practices.

---

# Safety Philosophy

Safety is a condition of employment.

Operational performance will never take priority over safety.

---

# Long-Term Objective

Develop an industry-leading safety culture that supports operational excellence while reducing accidents, injuries, and preventable losses.
