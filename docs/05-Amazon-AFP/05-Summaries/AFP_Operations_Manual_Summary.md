# Executive Summary

The Amazon Freight Partner (AFP) Program is designed to create a transportation network that combines Amazon freight demand with independently owned trucking businesses.

Amazon provides:

- Consistent freight
- Branded tractors
- Maintenance support
- Technology platforms
- Operational coaching
- Safety resources

AFP Owners are responsible for:

- Business operations
- Driver hiring
- Compliance
- Payroll
- Safety culture
- Performance management

Amazon audits partners for compliance but does not operate the AFP business.

---

# AFP Success Model

AFP growth is driven by three primary areas:

1. Service Performance
2. Safety Performance
3. Compliance Performance

Failure in any area can delay growth and expansion.

# Safety Program

Amazon safety philosophy:

Safety is integral to everything.

AFP Expectations:

- Establish safety culture
- Hire qualified drivers
- Maintain safety training
- Investigate incidents
- Monitor safety technology

Recommended Driver Standards:

- 2+ years CDL experience
- No serious moving violations
- No more than 3 minor violations in 3 years

# FMCSA BASIC Monitoring

Amazon monitors:

- Unsafe Driving
- Hours of Service
- Driver Fitness
- Controlled Substance / Alcohol
- Vehicle Maintenance

Thresholds:

- Unsafe Driving: 60
- HOS: 60
- Driver Fitness: 75
- Controlled Substance: 75
- Vehicle Maintenance: 75

Poor BASIC scores can impact AFP growth eligibility.

---

# Yard Safety Requirements

Mandatory:

- Safety vest
- 5 MPH speed limit
- No red dock door hookups
- No tailgating
- Report all incidents
- Maintain 20-foot clearance from moving equipment

Driver Requirements:

- Tug test
- Kingpin verification
- Parking brake engagement
- Key retention

---

# Incident Reporting

Emergency:

- Call 911

Amazon Reporting:

Accident Reporting Center (ARC)

856-333-6510

Relay Callback Process:

1. Open Relay
2. Request Callback
3. Select Issue Type
4. Await ROC Contact

All incidents must be reported regardless of fault.

---

# Driver Recruiting Strategy

Recommended:

- Begin recruiting 4-6 weeks before launch
- Maintain approximately 2 drivers per tractor
- Hire ahead of attrition

Recruiting Pipeline:

Applicants
→ Interviews
→ Offers
→ Compliance Checks
→ Driver Qualification Files
→ Hire
